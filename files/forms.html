Application Procedure and Forms

Please Follow the Directions given below to know what to do.


Are you looking to send your child to WUNS?

  • You must fill this form out to apply for admission. This form only needs to be filled out once. Application Form

Has your child been accepted into WUNS?

  • Fill out the Developmental History Form (only needs to be done once). Developmental History Form

  • Fill out the Enrollment Form. Enrollment Form

  • Fill out the two medical forms which are only available as PDFs and must also be filled out and returned.

    Child Medical Examination Report

    Immunization Consent/History




Is your child a returning student to WUNS?

  • The Enrollment Form needs to be filled out every year that your child attends WUNS. Enrollment Form

  • Fill out the two medical forms which are only available as PDFs and must also be filled out and returned.

    Child Medical Examination Report

    Immunization Consent/History
